How it runs

One regulatory event, end-to-end.

Here's what happens from the moment the FCA publishes a new consultation to the day your audit package exports.

Step 01 · T+0 min

Captured

Horizon Scanning ingests the publication. Native-language capture, normalised, deduplicated.

Step 02 · T+20 min

Classified

Analyst desk reviews, scores impact, writes brief, links to thread. Tags taxonomy + framework.

Step 03 · T+30 min

Routed

Routing rules fire. Task created, owner assigned, SLA set, stakeholder notified via preferred channel.

Step 04 · T+5 days

Evidenced & closed

Owner completes acceptance criteria, uploads evidence, reviewer signs off. Trail locked.
