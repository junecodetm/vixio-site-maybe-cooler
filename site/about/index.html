Our story

Twenty years translating regulation into clarity.

We brought together experts in regulation, law, and compliance to give clients the intelligence they need to anticipate and navigate the global gambling and payments regulatory landscape — a mandate that's only grown wider as financial services and digital assets have converged.

What started as a desk of analysts reading rules has become a platform where real-time data, human analysis, and workflow meet — so compliance teams can go from reactive triage to orchestrated operations.

We work with global operators, regulators, payment institutions, banks, suppliers, and the law firms that advise them. Headquartered with offices in London and Washington DC (opened in 2010), with an analyst desk across multiple time zones.
