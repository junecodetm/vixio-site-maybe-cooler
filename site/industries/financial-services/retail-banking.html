The double bind

Firefighting vs. faster launches — the retail banking squeeze.

Retail banks face a double bind: compliance firefighting consumes capacity while the business needs quicker product iterations and market entry. Manual workflows, email tasking, and spreadsheet tracking slow approvals, raise error risk, and make evidencing outcomes harder. Vixio replaces that with a single trusted source across PSD3/PSR and open banking, Consumer Duty outcomes, financial crime and sanctions, operational resilience (DORA), data/privacy regulations, and emerging AI rules.
