For digital assets

The frameworks that matter.

MiCA

ART, EMT, CASP regimes, whitepaper requirements, reserve composition, marketing rules and ESMA/EBA Level-2 measures. National application tracked via BaFin, AMF, CSSF, CBI and CONSOB.

FATF Travel Rule

FATF Recommendation 16, EU Transfer of Funds Regulation (TFR), US FinCEN guidance, UK MLRs, MAS and HKMA expectations — globally mapped.

Tokenisation & CBDCs

eIDAS 2, EU DLT Pilot Regime, wholesale CBDC experiments, retail CBDC policy at ECB and BoE, private-settlement layers.

VASP registration & licensing

FCA cryptoasset registration, MAS PSA, NYDFS BitLicense, MiCA CASP authorisation, MAS DPT, FINMA, ADGM and VARA — checklist-driven.

Promotions & marketing

UK FCA financial-promotion regime, EU MiCA marketing rules, SEC guidance, SFC, MAS DPT prohibitions — one view of what you can say, where.

Custody, market integrity & stablecoins

Custody segregation, proof-of-reserves, stablecoin reserve composition, market abuse under MiCA, SEC/CFTC approaches and US GENIUS-style proposals.
