★ Lead prediction · 2026

The first material DORA enforcement lands — and it's about third-party concentration.

2025 was DORA's quiet implementation year. 2026 is when supervisors stop reading register submissions and start acting on them. We expect the first material enforcement to centre not on incident reporting failure — but on undisclosed concentration in critical ICT third-party providers. The reaction will reshape vendor strategy across the Eurozone.

JR
James Reilly Head of Analyst Desk · Vixio
